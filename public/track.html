CIRCUIT NOTES

🏁
Layout
Technical flowing circuit with a mix of fast sweepers and tight chicanes. The back section features a demanding S-complex that rewards smooth driving.
🔑
Key Corners
Main hairpin at the far end of the circuit is the primary overtaking opportunity. The infield chicanes require precise braking and early apex.
⚠️
Watch Out For
The track can be slippery after rain — especially the painted kerbing. Allow extra braking distance in wet conditions.
🕐
Typical Lap Time
25–35 seconds for 1/8 GT depending on setup and conditions

GET HERE

🚗

By Car

Enter St Anne's Park via the main entrance on Mount Prospect Avenue, Raheny. Follow the road into the park — the circuit is visible to the left past the rose garden.

🚂

By DART

Take the DART to Raheny station. Exit onto Howth Road and walk through St Anne's Park — approximately 10 minutes on foot to the circuit.

🚌

By Bus

Bus routes 29A, 31, and 32 all serve Raheny and the Howth Road. The park's main entrance is a short walk from the bus stops.

🅿️

Parking

Free parking in the main St Anne's Park car park. On race days, arrive early as spaces fill up. Additional street parking available on nearby residential roads.
